Basically, The Undominant refers to activity or anything that you wouldn't normally do as it goes against your typical routine or your dominant skills (aka. things you are good at or comfortable doing). For example, in everyday life, you might eat using fork. The Undominant would challenge you to try eating with hands or chopsticks only. From this idea, I challenge myself to do the Undominant quest each week, and I hope you enjoy my struggle :)
