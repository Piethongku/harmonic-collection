Bored of the high resolution?

this challenge, watched hours of low resolution content on Youtube, which I chose one of Cartoon Network’s video from 2007 with a resolution of 480p.

Review's Here!
This might be my most favourite challenge so far. I mean aside from getting to watch my favourtie childhood cartoon, it also brought me back a lot of comforting feeling. Surprisingly, watching hours of low resolution video is not as difficult as I thought, or even if it was difficult at all. Because, today, the media all aims to have the highest image quality with higher level of detailed, so I thought that maybe my brain would have already adapted to that and this challenge would be a suffer. However, it turned out that the low resolution was very much more eye-soothing! I love the experience and how it made me feel and I challenge you to try the same too :)
