Newspaper > Tiktok

They are so different.

For me, Tiktok (and Twitter) is my main source to keep up with the trend and to know what is happening. But, after I read the Newspaper, it's like the trend for another different world. I thought I know "what is happening" but I realised now that it's not true. All I have been doing is the mindless scrolling , wasting my time on the "trend" I thought was important to keep posted, but should I really care about the "genZ" trend if I don't even know what's really going on around the world?

However, for the sake of my socialising skills, Tiktok is unevitable. It really helps me connecting with others a lot easier and many creative ideas for my projects are from the inspirations I found on Tiktok. It's undeniable that this app really opens opportunity for everyone to have their own spot and to be more open to endless ideas.
What I have learned from this Undominant challenge is to balance the sources of my input. Be even more open to all kind of media even the old ones.
