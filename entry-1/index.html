left hand & drawing

my favourite (things): edition

As it is.

Drawing with your dominant hand is challenging enough, but have you ever tried doing it with your non-dominant hand? It's even harder, especially when you're drawing things, people, or places you really love. We all want our favorite things to be perfect, don't we? But what if we tried to create them with our non-dominant hand? It could be a fun challenge that teaches us to appreciate imperfections and enjoy things just as they are. Why not give it a try?
